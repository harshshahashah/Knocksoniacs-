eBAJA 2024

SAEINDIA is an affiliate society of SAE International, registered as an Indian non-profit engineering and scientific society dedicated to the advancement of the mobility community in India. As an individual member driven society of mobility practitioners, SAEINDIA comprises members associated with transforming the transportation industry - which includes engineers, executives from the industry, government officials, academics and students. Principal emphasis is placed on industries such as automotive, aerospace and commercial vehicles. SAEINDIA promotes and undertakes initiatives for knowledge dissemination and advancement in mobility technologies catering to land, sea, air and space. Out of many student centered events, BAJA SAEINDIA is one of the grandest event falling under the umbrella of SAEINDIA.The buggies are evaluated on various factors such as cost, aesthetics, and the quality of the fabrication, as part of the static evaluation. The business event is further conducted, which exhibits the creativity level and business approach of the teams. There are also many dynamic tests such as acceleration, Braking, maneuverability over all-terrain, to evaluate the manufactured ATV. But none of these tests are more grueling than the endurance race where the drivers and buggies are pushed to their limits till a winner emerges.

Team Knocksoniacs, comprising of 25 undergraduate students from School of Mechanical Sciences have participated in eBAJA Student India 2024 which would be held at Banglore or Pithampur. The team would be designing and manufacturing an electric all terrain vehicle. The preliminary phase is concluded and the team has entered the virtual event to be held in November 2023.
