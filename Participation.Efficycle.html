EFFICYCLE

“EFFICYCLE” derived from Efficient-Cycle promotes the objective of providing oppurtunity to the students to conceive, design and fabricate a three wheel configuration vehicle powered by human-electric hybrid power and capable of seating two passengers catering to the day to day mobility needs. The vehicle must be aerodynamic, engineered for performance & safety and ergonomically designed. The objective is to promote innovation and generate consciousness amongst the young engineers towards environment friendly mobility solution.

Team Knocksoniacs Participated in Efficycle 2022 in the advanced format. The team designed and manufactured a hybrid three wheel vehicle. Hybrid in the sense that it can be powered manually as well as electrically and both simultaneously as well as one at a time.
The team consisted of twelve third year undergraduate that time. They designed the vehicle in about 4 months and took similar period for manufacturing. The event was hoisted by Lovely Professional University, Phagwara, Punjab from 22nd Nov to 25th Nov 2022.
Team Knocksoniacs went ahead to the final testing in LPU, Punjab after clearing the online inspection test. Knocksoniacs was placed at 7th position among teams participating all across the nation in advanced category.
